Why Join the Taekwondo Summer Camp 2026?

Looking for the ultimate summer training experience? The Summer Mountain Taekwondo Training Camp in Karpenisi, Greece, is your gateway to elite-level training, international connections, and lifelong memories.

📍

Location & Dates

Held in beautiful Karpenisi from 20–25 August 2026 — a mountain destination ideal for high-performance training in a refreshing summer climate.

🥋

What's Included

  • Daily high-intensity training sessions
  • Expert coaching for Kids, Cadets, Juniors & Seniors
  • Special seminars for coaches & assistants
  • Competition prep for G1, G2 & international events
🌍

Who Can Join

Athletes and teams from Greece, Cyprus, the Balkans, across Europe and all over the world are welcome. Train, connect, and grow with practitioners from everywhere.
