🥋 About the Camp

Welcome to the Taekwondo Summer Camp in Karpenisi! Join us from August 21st to 26th, 2025, for an unforgettable training experience in the heart of the Greek mountains.

Train with elite coaches and experienced athletes in twice-daily sessions, including sparring, skill work, and more.

🏨 Stay at a beautiful hotel with 3 meals a day and enjoy extra seminars on sports nutrition and athlete psychology.

This is more than just a camp — it’s a step forward in your Taekwondo journey. 🌟
