About the Camp

Welcome to the Taekwondo Summer Camp in Karpenisi! Join us from August 21st to 26th, 2025, for an unforgettable training experience in the heart of the Greek mountains. Train with elite-level coaches and experienced athletes, with twice-daily sessions, including sparring, skill development, and more. Enjoy all-inclusive hotel accommodation with three daily meals, and take part in special seminars on sports nutrition and athlete psychology. This is more than just a camp — it’s a step forward in your Taekwondo journey.
